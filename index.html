Simple Social Buttons

A bunch of button styles for social network links, with Font Awesome icons.

Current version: 0.1
/simpleSocialButtons @nicklleite /nicholasleite /nicklleite

A collection of social buttons, that can be almost 100% customizable (the goal is to reach the 100%). All the social buttons out there are less custom and that creates a need to "photoshop" a new one, with your needs and words. The SSB collection solves that with text-based icons (FontAwesome) and the option to choose what text you want in the button.

24 butons - 5 sizes

By default, all the buttons come in 5 different sizes, each with its respective color and icon. (I've said that all the buttons are flat?)

Imaginaaatioon

By downloading the package, your imagination is the limit when it comes to the customization of buttons.
